How It Works

Simple, sacred, and deeply personal.

🗓️

1. Choose Your Track

Select a 3, 7, or 21-day journey based on where you are in your faith walk right now.

📖

2. Open Each Day

Each day has a theme, scripture, reflection prompts, a prayer guide, and a personal declaration.

🔒

3. Journal Privately

Write freely in your private journal. Notes save to your device only — never shared with anyone.

Your Reflections Belong to You

We believe your prayer journal is sacred and private. Everything you write stays on your own device — no servers, no accounts, no data collection. Facilitators never see your personal reflections.

  • No account or sign-up required to use the journal
  • All journal data stored locally in your browser only
  • Sharing in community groups is always optional
  • You own and control every word you write
