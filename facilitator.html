🏛️ Church Facilitator Guide

Everything you need to lead your congregation through a powerful prayer and fasting journey.

Welcome, Facilitator

This guide is designed for pastors, small group leaders, and ministry coordinators who want to lead their congregation through the Digital Prayer Companion journey. Your role is essential — not to manage people's private reflections, but to create an environment where participants feel safe, encouraged, and supported as they seek God.

Your Core Responsibilities

🤝

Welcome & Onboard

Warmly welcome every participant regardless of where they are in their faith journey. Send the Day 0 welcome message (below) before the journey begins.

📖

Share Daily Scripture

Post the day's scripture and theme in the group each morning. You can use the scripts provided in this guide for each day.

💬

Encourage Consistently

A simple word of encouragement each day goes a long way. You don't need to write sermons — a genuine, brief message is enough.

🔐

Protect Privacy Always

Never pressure anyone to share their personal reflections. Never share what participants tell you privately. This is non-negotiable.

WhatsApp Group Structure

Group Name Who It's For Purpose
📣 Announcements All participants Broadcast-only updates, reminders, and daily scriptures. No replies required.
🌿 3-Day Reset Group 3-day fast participants Daily encouragement for Days 1–3. Focused and brief.
🙏 7-Day Foundation Group 7-day fast participants Daily encouragement for Days 1–7. Slightly deeper engagement.
👑 21-Day Journey Group 21-day fast participants Full journey support. Optional sharing, weekly check-ins, prayer requests.

Day 0 — Welcome Script

🙏 Welcome to our Prayer & Fasting Journey!


We are so glad you're here. Over the next [3/7/21] days, we will be seeking God together through prayer, fasting, and daily reflection.


Here's what you need to know:

📖 Each day has a theme, scripture, and reflection guide — accessible on the Digital Prayer Companion website.

🔒 Your journal is completely private. It saves only to your own device. You are never required to share your personal reflections with anyone.

💬 This group is a safe space. Sharing is optional and always welcomed — but never pressured.

⏰ I'll post a daily scripture each morning at [time]. Feel free to respond, react, or simply receive it in silence.


Let's begin this journey with open hearts and expectant faith. God is going to move. 🙌

— [Your Name], Facilitator

Daily Message Templates

Copy and customise these for each day. Keep them brief — the companion website has the full content.

🤲 Day 1 — Surrender & Alignment

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and do not lean on your own understanding." — Proverbs 3:5

Today we begin by releasing control. Open today's reflection on the companion site and let God meet you there. You are not alone in this. 🙏

🌟 Day 3 — Clarity & Direction

"If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask God, who gives generously." — James 1:5

Three days in! You are doing amazing. Today, ask God boldly for direction. He is faithful to answer. 🙌

🎶 Day 7 — Gratitude & Praise

"Give thanks in all circumstances." — 1 Thessalonians 5:18

One full week! Celebrate this milestone. Today is about counting God's goodness. What has He done that deserves your praise today? 🙏

👑 Day 21 — Dedication & Commissioning

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations." — Matthew 28:19

YOU DID IT. 21 days of seeking God. Today we celebrate what He has done and commission you into what comes next. This is not an ending — it is a launching. Share something God did in you this journey. 🔥

Facilitator Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do

  • Post daily scriptures consistently
  • Celebrate milestones (Day 7, Day 14, Day 21)
  • Pray for participants by name privately
  • Acknowledge and affirm those who share voluntarily
  • Check in with anyone who seems to have gone silent
  • Model vulnerability by sharing your own journey briefly

❌ Don't

  • Pressure anyone to share reflections publicly
  • Share another participant's private disclosures
  • Make participants feel guilty for missing a day
  • Compare participants' experiences with each other
  • Post multiple messages per day — keep it focused
  • Respond to every message — let community build naturally

A Word to You, Facilitator

You are doing kingdom work. Your consistency, your prayers, and your presence matter more than you know. Thank you for creating a space where people can encounter God. Go well. 🙏
