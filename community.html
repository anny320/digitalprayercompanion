💬 Community Guide

How our WhatsApp community works — and how to get the most from it.

You Don't Have to Journey Alone

Faith grows in community. While your personal reflections and journal are entirely private, we've created a supportive WhatsApp community structure where participants can receive daily encouragement, share optional prayer requests, and walk this journey together. Participation in the community groups is always optional — and your personal journal is never shared.

Community Group Structure

📣
All Participants

Announcements Group

A broadcast-only group for general updates, daily scripture reminders, and community-wide encouragements. No replies required.

  • Daily 6:00 AM scripture reminder
  • Journey milestone celebrations
  • Important community updates
🌿
3-Day Fast

Reset Group (Days 1–3)

A focused group for participants on the 3-Day Reset track. Short, focused, and encouraging.

  • Theme and verse for each day
  • Celebrate your Day 3 completion
  • Optional: continue to 7 or 21 days
🙏
7-Day Fast

Foundation Group (Days 1–7)

For participants doing the 7-Day Foundation Fast. Slightly more depth with mid-week check-in.

  • Daily theme and verse
  • Midpoint encouragement on Day 4
  • Day 7 celebration and testimony space
👑
21-Day Fast

Full Journey Group (Days 1–21)

The full 21-day community with weekly rhythm, optional prayer requests, and final commissioning celebration.

  • Daily scripture at 6:00 AM
  • Weekly check-ins (Day 7, 14, 21)
  • Optional prayer request sharing
  • Testimony space on Day 20–21

Daily Rhythm

TimeWhat Happens

📝 AnytimeWrite in your private journal at your own pace

🙏 EveningSpeak your day's declaration before you sleep

Our Community Values

🔒

Privacy First

Personal reflections are never required to be shared. Your journal is yours alone.

🤝

Grace, Not Guilt

Missed a day? Come back. Every day is a fresh start. This is not a performance.

💛

Encouragement Only

No debate, no criticism, no unsolicited advice. Only love, prayer, and encouragement.
